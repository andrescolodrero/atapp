ATApp

ATApp was developed do give Aviation Technicans a tool to quickly and easily access information about ATA codes for free on all devices.

All the ATA information contained in this app is based on and compiled from the "FEDERAL AVIATION ADMINISTRATION JOINT AIRCRAFT SYSTEM/COMPONENT CODE TABLE AND DEFINITIONS" Update: 27.Okt 2008 byt the FAA.

ALWAYS USE APPLICABLE UP TO DATE MANUALS WHEN WORKING ON AIRCRAFT.

ATApp is a bi-product of the web service  www.mymxlog.com

mymxlog.com  provides aviation technicians and companies emploeing them the tool to track recent experience, continious training and schedule reminders and produce great reports from the system when required easily online around the world.
